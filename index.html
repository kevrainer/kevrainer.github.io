Web Development

I was introduced to web development in late 2016 when I stumbled upon a short film from Code.org. At the time, I pretty much knew nothing about coding (aside from the basic HTML taught in college). The below video was the first of the many that has sparked my interest in coding.

At the time I have a full time job and was busy managing my side business that really kept me occupied, having left with little to no free time for myself. For some reason though, I still felt compelled to learn and educate myself about code. So I made the decision to learn as much as I can in whatever spare time I have. After a couple days of research I decided to sign up with Treehouse. This marked the start of my journey on learning Web Development.
